Usage Notes

Serif (Playfair Display)

Voice & Authority: Used for primary narratives, heritage-focused messaging, and high-level structure. Playfair evokes a sense of editorial prestige and institutional stability.

  • • Hero Headers
  • • Section Headlines
  • • Brand Statements

Sans (DM Sans)

Utility & Legibility: Employed for dense information, functional elements, and interactive components. DM Sans provides the geometric precision needed for modern digital interfaces.

  • • Body Text & Reports
  • • UI Components & Buttons
  • • Data Visualizations

Architectural Feel

"The deliberate use of negative tracking in Display styles and generous tracking in Labels creates a rhythmic, structured appearance reminiscent of blueprint typography."
